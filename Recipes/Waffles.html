Hearty Waffles

yield: six waffles

2 cup flour
0.5 cup quick oats
0.25 cup flax meal
2 tablespoon brown sugar
4 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon nutmeg
1 cup almond milk
0.5 cup apple sauce
2 count eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
splash cream
2.5 tablespoon vegetable oil
pan spray
1. Whisk dries together in a big bowl.
2. Spray waffle iron generously with pan spray.
3. Add milk, apple sauce, eggs, vanilla, cream, and vegetable oil to same bowl and stir.
4. Fill waffle iron with batter to slightly overflowing, then quickly close the lid.
5. Cook time will vary depending on the heat of your waffle iron.  Look for a dark golden brown color.
