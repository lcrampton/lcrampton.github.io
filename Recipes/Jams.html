Jam

yield: one quart

fruit
sugar
apple cider vinegar
pectin (You can use the pectin abundant in apple skins if you purée them.)
1. Grind or blend your fruit.
2. If using dry pectin (powder), whisk with sugar.
3. Boil all ingredients together until reduced to something slightly more viscous than syrup.
4. Allow to cool and refrigerate.
