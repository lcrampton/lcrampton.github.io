Travel to Mozambique!

Logistics

Lodging

You really have one option here as an American tourist and it is Fatima's.

Transportation

Use taxis and tuk-tuks to get around Maputo. They can be a fun, quick, and thrilling mode of transport.

Use buses to get between cities and towns. These will feel cramped and hot. And the rides will last a looong time.

Ride a sailboat around Tofo Beach! You can pay about 2500 meticais at the Nest to take a dhow over to Survivor's island (population 80). There you can tour a small settlement, collect shells, and enjoy a feast.

Safety

Stay safe. Bring a padlock/Masterlock.

Languages Spoken

You would do well to speak some of these. You can certainly get by with either Portugese, which Mozambicans learn in school, or English, which many Mozambicans learn from television, the internet, and tourists.
  1. Português
  2. KiTonga - no Inhambane e Gaza
  3. Inglês
  4. Shangaan - perto de Maputo
